Vehicle Types

This scene shows the distribution of the different types of electric vehicles (battery-powered and plug-in hybrid) in the dataset. Hover over each segment of the pie chart to see detailed information about the vehicle type, including the count and percentage of each type.

I included this chart to provide a clear visual representation of the most common and least common types of electric vehicles. This helps in understanding the market distribution and popularity of various vehicle types.

One can hover over the pie chart to see the specific percentage and absolute # distributions of the types of cars
