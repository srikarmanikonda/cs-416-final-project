Base MSRP

This scene shows a bar chart representing the base MSRP (Manufacturer's Suggested Retail Price) of various electric vehicle models. The chart highlights both the affordability and premium segments within the electric vehicle market.

Understanding the price range of electric vehicles is crucial as it directly impacts consumer adoption. Lower-priced models can make electric vehicles more accessible to a broader audience, while higher-priced models often come with advanced features and longer ranges.

The chart also includes a dotted line representing the average price of all the vehicles in the dataset. This average price line helps to contextualize where each vehicle stands in terms of cost, providing a quick visual reference for identifying which models are above or below the average price.

Including this information allows us to analyze the market positioning of different electric vehicle models and understand the economic factors influencing consumer choices.
